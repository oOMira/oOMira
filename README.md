## What the FAQ

Here you'll find a collection of projects I am working on at the moment. I believe that coding is a form of creative expression and software should be fun to use. That's why they are all about topics close to my heart. If you need an onboarding you've already failed (in most cases). As a user you want to feel taken seriously and not like a child who has everything explained to them.

The last few months I tried out a bunch of ideas to see which ones would work out. So most of the projects are WIPs. I'm going to refine the ones that stood out to me and release them soon. Stay tuned for updates.
